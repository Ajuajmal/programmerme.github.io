Inaugural Session - Programmer Me

Today Programmer Me officially started its work. The student developers reached early to attend the 30 minute talk session.

To our surprise about 50 students from different branches such as CS,EC,IT,EEE and ME ranging from 1st years to 3rd-years were present.

After the registration we started our program with an interesting talk by Zameel (Co-ordinater) and followed by talks from Manoj Kumar Sir and Ajmal

From The talks

Zameel gave an introduction talk on "How to become a top programmer" by sharing his interesting experiences and views. "How a community can help us" discussed by Ajmal was an interesting talk on community. He also shared some opportunities available for the upcoming developers.
